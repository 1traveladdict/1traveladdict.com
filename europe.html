ATM fees can be very steep always use a credit card from a credit union, Fidelity Investments or Charles Swab as they refund your fees when you ATM use their cards. Depositing funds into a cash card with these companies lets you travel without carrying a lot of cash with you.

Taking a credit card with you make sure its a card card that does not charge foreign transaction fees.
